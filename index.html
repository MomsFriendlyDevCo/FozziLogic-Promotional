Streamlined Solutions for
NDIS Practitioners.

FozziLogic is a powerful online platform tailored for NDIS service providers. It enables practitioners to securely create, store, and access important client documents — such as risk assessments, progress notes, and care plans — anytime, anywhere. With streamlined tools and an easy-to-use interface, FozziLogic helps practitioners focus less on paperwork and more on delivering quality care.

Smarter Risk Assessments,
Less Time Wasted

FozziLogic shows each practitioner only what they need — when they need it.

Risk assessments can be lengthy, often exceeding 50 pages. FozziLogic streamlines this process by displaying only the relevant sections for each practitioner, eliminating the need to sift through unnecessary details.

The system also makes it easy to track who is responsible for each task, monitor due dates, and see when the next assessment period is approaching. Team leaders get a clear overview of work progress, making coordination and accountability effortless.
